About SafeHer

SafeHer is a web-based women’s safety prototype designed to provide quick and practical emergency support through SOS alerts, fake calls, and Guardian Mode. This prototype emphasizes speed, accessibility, and simplicity, and demonstrates the logic behind a real safety tool.
